Shilpa Chowbey

I am pursuing a B.S. in Cognitive and Computer Science, expecting to graduate in June of 2025 from University of California, San Diego. My degree specializes in Human-Computer Interaction. Currently, some of my interest are front-end development, full-stack, UI/UX engineering, and software engineering. I initially was introduced to many of these paths through IBM's student accelerate program. My career journey has also been influenced by classes such "Art of Product Management", "Advanced Data Structures", and "Cyborgs Now and In the Future." Through participating in school and extracurricular projects, I have gained experience and exposure to the design process, as evidenced by my case studies.

Outside of academics, I am the co-president of UCSD's student health educators, an associate of Triton Consulting Group, and a performer in Comfort Tones (an on-campus acapella group). I am also passionate about education, particularly after realizing the importance of diverse teachers. I have worked as an ACT tutor, TAed for multiple data science classes, and even worked as a coding instuctor for a Vanderbilt University summer camp!
